Based on Eq. 17: S_CO₂(t) = C(t−t₀) · γc · αc · ξc · Σφᵢ(1−Dᵢ)·Daᵢ. Simplification assumes γc=0.85 (concrete fraction), Dᵢ=22.7% initial carbonation. Carbonation degrees per mode from Table 1.
